09 — The business engine

From eyeballs
to advisors.

The channel sells two things: free playbooks (lead magnet) and one paid course. Both feed qualified leads to vetted human advisors. Here's the in-product surface.
Free playbook · 14 pages

Get the $1,847 most W-2 filers miss.

Punch in a couple things. We'll match you with a fee-only advisor in your state if — and only if — your situation is worth their time.

We don't sell your data. We do introduce you to one advisor — your call whether to book. Fine Print Finance receives a referral fee. That's the whole deal.

Qualified leads matched to a fee-only fiduciary advisor in their state.

Earn

Flat referral fee per booked consult. Channel stays free for the audience.

10 — Voice & tone

Write like the
smart older sibling.

Direct address. Real dollar amounts. Conversational fragments. Never the bank. Never the guru.

Sounds like us DO

"Your bank pays you 0.01%. The Fed pays them 5.3%. That's the gap."
"Sound familiar? Here's why."
"You are not bad with money. You were never shown the playbook."
"$340. That's what most filers leave on the table by skipping one form."
"That's the deal."

Sounds wrong DON'T

"Leverage synergies to optimize your financial wellness journey."
"Get RICH overnight with this one weird trick."
"Securities offered through… past performance does not guarantee…"
"As a savvy investor, you already know that diversification…"
"Click here to unlock the secret strategy the elites use!"
