## BRAND IDENTITY SNAPSHOT

**Channel name:** Fine Print Finance
**Host:** Josh
**Tagline:** *"The money was always there. Nobody showed you where to look."*
**Mission:** Decode the financial fine print every industry hides from regular people — taxes, investing, healthcare, real estate, banking — and show exactly where the money is.
**Tone:** Fired-up older sibling. Approachable. Never condescending. Genuinely on your side.
**Parent brand relationship:** Fine Print Finance carries a subtle visual DNA connection to BLOOM Ecosystem through shared coral/pink gradient — visible to those who know, invisible to those who don't.

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Wordmark Fonts
- **"Fine Print"** — Engravers MT or similar serif engraving font with currency/money texture overlay. The letterforms should feel like they were pressed from a printing plate. If exact font unavailable: use a bold serif (Playfair Display Black or Libre Baskerville Bold) with a green currency texture applied in Canva or Photoshop.
- **"Finance"** — Bold sans-serif, wide tracking. Montserrat ExtraBold or Bebas Neue. Apply the coral-to-pink gradient fill.

### Content Fonts (everything that isn't the logo)

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / Headlines** | Montserrat | ExtraBold 800 | Video titles, section headers, thumbnail text |
| **Subheadlines** | Montserrat | SemiBold 600 | Section intros, card headers |
| **Body Copy** | Plus Jakarta Sans | Regular 400 | Scripts, descriptions, captions |
| **Body Emphasis** | Plus Jakarta Sans | SemiBold 600 | Key terms, stat callouts |
| **Tagline** | Montserrat | Bold 700 | Always two lines, white + coral split |
| **Pill Bar / Labels** | Montserrat | Medium 500 | Bottom bar icons and labels |

### Fallback Stack (if primary fonts unavailable)
```
Display: 'Montserrat', 'Arial Black', sans-serif
Body: 'Plus Jakarta Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif
```

## LOGO USAGE RULES

### Primary Logo
- "Fine Print" stacked above "Finance"
- "Fine Print" in currency engraving style, dark green
- "Finance" in coral-to-pink gradient, bold sans-serif
- Tagline below in two-tone: white + coral

### Logo Variations Needed
1. **Full lockup** — stacked with tagline (primary, use on banner/headers)
2. **Compact lockup** — stacked without tagline (use on thumbnails, profile images)
3. **Horizontal lockup** — "Fine Print Finance" on one line (use on watermarks, lower thirds)
4. **Icon mark** — stylized "$" or magnifying glass over fine print text (use as favicon, social avatar, app icon)
5. **Dark version** — full lockup for use on light backgrounds
6. **Light version** — full lockup for use on dark backgrounds

### Clear Space Rule
Always maintain padding equal to the height of the letter "F" in "Finance" on all four sides of the logo.

### Never Do
- Stretch or distort the wordmark
- Change the gradient direction
- Use the green currency style on "Finance" or the gradient on "Fine Print"
- Place on a busy background without a dark overlay or card behind it
- Use pure black or pure white as background — always use the warm versions

## DESIGN SYSTEM

### Buttons & CTAs
```
Primary CTA:
  Background: gradient #F4A261 → #E76F8B at 135deg
  Text: #FFFFFF, Montserrat Bold
  Border radius: 8px
  Padding: 12px 24px

Secondary CTA:
  Background: transparent
  Border: 1.5px solid #F4A261
  Text: #F4A261, Montserrat SemiBold
  Border radius: 8px

Dark pill (bottom bar style):
  Background: #1A1714
  Text: #F5F3F0, Montserrat Medium
  Icon: gradient fill
  Border radius: 32px (fully rounded)
  Padding: 10px 20px
```

### Cards
```
Light card:
  Background: #F5F3F0
  Border: 1px solid #E8E4E0
  Border radius: 12px
  Shadow: 0 2px 12px rgba(26,23,20,0.06)

Dark card:
  Background: #2A2420
  Border: 1px solid #3A3330
  Border radius: 12px
```

### Accent Lines / Underlines
- Use coral-to-pink gradient for underline emphasis (as seen on "Finance" in the banner)
- Chalk/marker style underlines preferred over clean rules — keeps the educational, handwritten whiteboard energy
- Thickness: 3-4px for section underlines, 6-8px for headline underlines

### Icons
- Style: filled circles with gradient background, white icon inside
- Gradient: `#F4A261 → #E76F8B`
- Border radius: fully rounded (pill shape for groups, circle for singles)
- Size: 32px minimum for readability

## PHOTOGRAPHY & CHARACTER DIRECTION

### Josh Character Style
- 3D animated / illustrated character — NOT photorealistic
- Warm, cozy interior setting — bookshelves, ambient lamp light, coffee mug
- Whiteboard visible behind him with financial concepts
- Expression: engaged, slightly raised eyebrow, approachable confidence
- Wardrobe: casual — hoodie or zip sweater over white tee, no suit, no tie
- Always feels like "the smart friend who explains things" not "the banker who sells you things"

### Background Environment
- Warm amber/brown tones — cozy study or home office
- Books visible (financial titles — intentional choices)
- Plants for warmth
- Candles or Edison bulbs for ambient light
- Whiteboard or corkboard in background with relevant content visible

### What to Avoid
- Corporate office settings
- Suits or formal wear
- Cold blue/grey color temperature
- Stock photo vibes
- Anything that feels like a bank advertisement

## VOICE & TONE (for design copy)

**DO use:**
- Short, punchy sentences
- Real numbers with dollar signs
- "You" language — direct address
- Conversational fragments — "That's the deal." "Here's why."
- Questions that create tension — "Sound familiar?"

**NEVER use:**
- Corporate financial disclaimers in hero copy (save for footnotes)
- Passive voice
- Jargon without immediate plain-English translation
- Hyperbole that sounds like a scam — "Get RICH overnight"
- Anything that sounds like an ad

## BLOOM ECOSYSTEM CONNECTION

Fine Print Finance is a BLOOM Ecosystem content property. The visual DNA connection is intentional but subtle:

| Element | BLOOM Colors | Fine Print Finance Colors |
|---|---|---|
| Primary gradient start | `#F4A261` Coral | `#F4A261` Coral — **identical** |
| Primary gradient end | `#E76F8B` Pink | `#E76F8B` Pink — **identical** |
| Finance accent | — | `#4A6741` Currency Green — **unique to FPF** |
| Background | Light grey | Warm charcoal + light grey — **shared base** |

The green is the differentiator. It signals finance/money specifically and creates visual separation from other BLOOM properties while keeping the gradient as the family tie.

**Do not:** reference BLOOM Ecosystem in any Fine Print Finance public-facing materials.
**Do:** ensure any designer working on BLOOM and FPF simultaneously can see the shared gradient DNA.
